About Us

EcomVision is a global comparison platform, providing unbiased comparisons across various categories: from tech products to cities. By providing accurate information in an easy-to-visualize way, we aim to simplify decision making.

The Problem:

Information Overload

Making the right choice when faced with an ever-growing number of options is challenging. Gathering relevant and accurate data is no easy task: our brains are constantly overloaded with information, offers, and possibilities. With new products and features to keep up with, making the choice that best suits one's needs demands a lot of time — and with possible bias from manufacturers and retailers, there is no guaranteed accuracy in what you'll find in your research.

Our Solution:

Fact-based, Objective Comparisons

We understand that making objective and well-informed decisions is a challenging task and that our users' time is precious. It's our mission to research, analyze, and present data in a straightforward way. Do you want a smartphone with an ultra-fast processor? Or are you planning a trip to a city with lots of museums? Whatever you’re looking for, all you have to do is to have your priorities in mind — EcomVision does the hard work for you.

FAQs

Which sources do you use for gathering data about items?

We use the official websites of manufacturers, product sheets, and product manuals to gather data about products. For apps, we usually do a bit of testing to get a better idea of the functionalities. When it comes to cities and ski resorts, we use official statistics presented by the local authorities. As for universities and faculties, we refer to their official websites and the statistics provided by the institutions. We always focus on the objective aspects and collect data that makes sense for our comparisons.

Which categories are available on your site?

As we are a startup, We have just 3 categories on our website those are Televisions, Smartphones and Laptops but we are bringing some new categories soon.

What is the use of Price Tracking button on Product page?

On EcomVision, the "Track Price" button lets users monitor a product’s price across multiple sellers. Once activated, it alerts you when the price drops or meets your desired target. You can customize notifications via email. This helps you buy at the best time by avoiding overpaying.

How do you make money?

Most of our revenue comes from affiliate marketing: when you buy a product through our links, we may earn a small commission. However, that doesn’t affect the Versus score or the visibility of a product. We also display non-invasive ads on our comparison pages.
